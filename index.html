PRODUCTIVITY TOOLS

USE THESE TOOLS TO MAKE USE OF YOUR TIME

  To-Do List

  With a to-do list tasks can be marked down to create a sense of organization and urgency to get those tasks complete and checked off, making room for more to be done.

  Pomodoro Clock

  The Pomodoro Clock is the classic technique of allotting 25 minutes to complete whatever task you may have. Through dedicating 25 minutes without pause to a task, focus can be put forth to good use and with breaks in between the 25 minute blocks, this may be the sustainable productivity tool to get that work done.

  Stopwatch

  A standard stopwatch to monitor the amount of time being passed, making sure those moments are being used to its maximum potential.

  Hour Blocks

  Through sectioning the day into 1 hour blocks, the tasks that must be done in a day to day basis are able to be illustrated so that time is not wasted thinking about what work comes next in the day, further helping people get the most out of their day.

  Time Budget

 Sometimes it helps to treat time like money and budget it accordingly. Knowing how we want to spend our time, like money, can prevent needless spending on days when progress may be made.
